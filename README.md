### Summary:
Comparing Electoral Systems for U.S. Presidential Elections

### Data:
Original sets from the FEC and U.S. Census Bureau are located in Original_files folder

### Methods:
Compared multiple ways of distributing Electoral College Votes to the popular vote percentage
to determine the methods holding most closely to the principles of democratic governance

### Results:
Final presentation viewable as PowerPoint file (PP_Capstone.pptx)

### Conclusions:
Restrictions of the Permanent Apportionment Act of 1929 and winner take all systems utilized by most states 
are problematic to democratic principles of governance.
